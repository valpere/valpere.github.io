## Overview

Led the Kyiv development team over 5+ years building core components for the PortaSwitch BSS, Billing, and Softswitch platform serving a diverse range of Communication Service Providers: MNO/MVNO/MVNE, cable/MSO, fixed-line, broadband, VoIP, satellite, and IoT providers.

Architected billing solutions on high-availability Linux infrastructure with MySQL/PostgreSQL databases and delivered enterprise-grade BSS solutions ensuring 99.9% system reliability for critical telecommunications infrastructure serving millions of end users globally.
